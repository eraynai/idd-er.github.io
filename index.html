What is Interaction Design?

The practice of designing interactive digital products, environments, systems, and services

Why George Brown?

What are you going to learn?

The interaction design program at George Brown College is a must for anyone who wants to move into the field of user design. In the program you learn everything from programs such as Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop to design thinking skills that will serve you well in your chosen career path.
